The Modern Times

Real Madrid started there modern era when the club won five consecutive European Cup titles between 1956 and 1960. This time was also called the golden era. They won many trophies and they improved financially during this period of time. Real Madrid continued to win trophies and they gradually turned into big club superpowers.
