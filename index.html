# History of Real Madrid - Page 1

History of Real Madrid

Page 1: Home Page

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol was founded on March 6, 1902, by a group of students at the Institución Libre de Enseñanza, a progressive educational institution in Madrid. The clubs first president was Julián Palacios, and the club is one of the biggest and most historic clubs today. Real Madrid is based in Madrid, Spain, and plays their home matches at the Santiago Bernabeu. The stadium has a capacity of 80,000. The clubs biggest rivals are Barcelona, and when the play against eacother the game is called El Classico.
